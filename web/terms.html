Program Name

Hey804 — Richmond City Services Information

What This Service Does

Hey804 provides automated information about Richmond, Virginia city services including taxes, benefits, utilities, and housing. It also delivers emergency alerts to opted-in subscribers. All information is sourced from official city and state websites.

How to Opt In

You opt in by sending any text message to the Hey804 phone number. Your first inbound message constitutes consent to receive automated replies and emergency alerts. You may also text START or HEY to opt in.

Message Frequency

You will receive one reply per question you send. Emergency alerts are sent as needed (typically rare — only during active city emergencies such as boil water advisories or severe weather).

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

Text STOP to the Hey804 number at any time. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages. You can also text CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or QUIT.

How to Get Help

Text HELP to the Hey804 number for support information, or call RVA 311 at 804-646-7000 (Mon-Fri 8am-7pm, Sat 9am-1pm).

Information Accuracy

Hey804 provides information sourced from official Richmond city (rva.gov) and Virginia state websites. Every answer includes source links so you can verify the information. However, this service does not make eligibility determinations — it helps you understand your options and tells you who to contact.

If you need official guidance on a specific case, contact the relevant department directly or call RVA 311.

Not a Substitute for Emergency Services

Hey804 is an information service. For life-threatening emergencies, call 911. For non-emergency city services, call 311 (804-646-7000).

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy for details on data collection, use, and retention. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Support Contact

This service was built for the Hack for RVA 2026 hackathon. For support, text HELP to the Hey804 number or visit rvahacks.org.
